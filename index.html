Christina Youn

I am a second year PhD student in computer science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Tarek Abdelzaher. Previously, I was an undergraduate at the University of Notre Dame where I conducted research in Dong Wang's social sensing lab and Christian Poellabauer's mobile computing lab.

I am primarily interested in social sensing, social mathematics, and information dynamics. This means that I often look at data from social media platforms such as Reddit and Twitter to analyze problems such as information propagation!
